Our Story

The Legacy Behind Ugomba-Noel LTD

At Ugomba-Noel LTD, we soar beyond ordinary standards. Named after the Igbo word 'Ugomba' — meaning 'the eagle and pride of a nation' — our company honours the legacy of Late Ugomba Noel, a visionary known for excellence, innovation, and community service.

We work diligently to elevate Nigeria's food security through excellence in vegetable oil production. Like an eagle soaring above challenges, we bring premium quality products to Nigerian families, hotels and restaurants.

01
Excellence Without Compromise

Highest standards using locally sourced Nigerian materials, ensuring purity in every bottle.

02
Community Impact

Working directly with local farmers, making premium oil accessible through competitive pricing.

03
Effective Innovation

Innovative production methods and quality improvements — letting our products speak for themselves.

04
Food Security

Building a legacy of food security and economic empowerment in Nigeria, one bottle at a time.

🎯

Our Mission

To deliver premium, pure vegetable oils while fostering food security in Nigeria through sustainable local sourcing and innovative production.

Our Production
